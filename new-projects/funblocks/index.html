How to Quickly Learn and Use FunBlocks AI Flow?

FunBlocks AI Flow Quick Start

FunBlocks AI Flow is a user-friendly and powerful AI application. You can get started in just a few steps.

1. Create a New Board:

Click "Board List" in the upper left corner and select "Create new board".

2. Chat with AI:

In the "AI Assistants List" on the left, select your desired AI assistant. Then, type your questions or ideas in the dialog box, just like chatting with a friend. The AI will respond in an easy-to-understand mind map format.

3. Continue Exploring:

Each time the AI responds, it will also generate questions or topics for further exploration. You can choose the direction you are interested in and continue to explore in depth.

4. Stay Curious:

With its user-friendly design, FunBlocks AI Flow is easy to learn and lets you combine curiosity with powerful AI to boost your creativity.

Experience the fun and simplicity of FunBlocks AI Flow. Dive into this exciting app today!
